About me

Hi, my name is Omar Massfih and I work as a Data Platform Engineer at Bama, where I build and operate the group's data platform on Azure and Snowflake. Day to day that means ingestion and transformation pipelines with Python, dlt and dbt, agentic AI solutions with LangGraph and LangChain, and infrastructure that is automated end to end with Terraform and CI/CD.

I hold a master's degree in informatics from the University of Oslo, and before Bama I spent two years at IBM building backend services, APIs and agent-based AI, and administering Kubernetes and OpenShift clusters, from initial setup to the services running on them. That mix of systems, data and full-stack work is what I keep coming back to.

What I enjoy most is the platform side of the work: the pipelines and infrastructure other teams build on without having to think about it. The 10 Azure and 6 Red Hat certifications came mostly from wanting to understand that layer properly.

On this website I share my projects and notes. Have a look around, and get in touch if you would like to know more!
